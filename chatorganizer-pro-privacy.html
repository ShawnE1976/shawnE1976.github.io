ChatOrganizer Pro - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Overview

ChatOrganizer Pro is a Chrome extension that helps you organize ChatGPT and Claude conversations into folders with tags and search. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

ChatOrganizer Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension does not track you, and does not use cookies or analytics.

Data Usage

The extension reads conversation data from ChatGPT and Claude interfaces to enable folder organization, tagging, and search. All organization data is stored locally on your device.

Data Stored Locally

  • Folder and Tag Data: Your folder structure, tags, and organization preferences are stored locally in Chrome's storage on your device.
  • Subscription Status: Your payment status is stored locally via ExtensionPay.

Third-Party Services

  • ExtensionPay: Used for subscription management. See ExtensionPay's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. All conversation organization data stays on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
