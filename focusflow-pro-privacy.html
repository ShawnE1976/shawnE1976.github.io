FocusFlow Pro - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Overview

FocusFlow Pro is a Chrome extension that provides a new tab dashboard with tasks, pomodoro timer, tab saver, and AI-powered task suggestions. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

FocusFlow Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension does not track you, and does not use cookies or analytics.

Data Usage

The extension stores your tasks, timer settings, and saved tabs locally on your device. AI-powered task suggestions are generated by sending task data to the Anthropic API (Claude AI).

Data Stored Locally

  • Tasks and Settings: Your tasks, timer preferences, and saved tabs are stored locally in Chrome's storage on your device.
  • API Key: Your Claude API key is stored locally in Chrome's storage. It is never transmitted to any server other than the Anthropic API.
  • Subscription Status: Your payment status is stored locally via ExtensionPay.

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic API: Task data may be sent to Anthropic's API for AI-powered suggestions. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • ExtensionPay: Used for subscription management. See ExtensionPay's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. All data stays on your device unless sent to the Anthropic API for task suggestions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
